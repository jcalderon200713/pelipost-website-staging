Facilities seeing real results

From large state prisons to county detention centers, Pelipost adapts to facilities of every size and type.

State Correctional Facility
Midwestern State Prison Complex
Challenge

A 3,200-bed facility processing over 1,400 pieces of mail per week manually. Staff reported an average of 18 staff-hours per week on mail inspection and a 12% return rate for rule violations.

Solution

Pelipost replaced all physical mail processing for personal photos and printed correspondence. Custom rules were configured to match the facility's existing mail policy exactly.

Outcome

Mail-related staff hours reduced to near zero. Contraband incidents involving mail dropped significantly in the first quarter after launch.

County Detention Center
Southeast Regional County Jail
Challenge

A 640-bed county facility with high resident turnover. Family members frequently violated mail rules unknowingly, generating grievances and consuming correctional officer time.

Solution

Pelipost was implemented with the jail's specific booking and housing unit rules encoded. Real-time notifications keep families informed of submission status instantly, reducing inbound calls to staff.

Outcome

Mail grievances related to rejected correspondence fell by over 80%. Family satisfaction improved markedly, and staff reported significantly fewer mail-related interruptions.
