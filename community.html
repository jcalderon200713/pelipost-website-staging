More than a mail service

Built with community, for community

Pelipost exists because of the families walking this journey together. These are the programs, partners, and initiatives that make that possible.

What we built

Support Circles

Find your people. Our Support Circles connect families who get it, because they're living it too. Whether you're a partner, a parent, or walking through this for the first time, there's a circle for you.

Global Incarcerated Loved One Day

Every August 8th, families around the world pause to honor their incarcerated loved ones. GILO Day is a holiday we created to make the invisible visible and remind every family: you are not alone.

Who we work with

We partner with organizations who share our belief that family connection changes lives.

Prison Fellowship

We partner with Prison Fellowship to help Angel Tree families stay connected with their incarcerated loved ones. Through this program, families receive vouchers for free Pelipost orders, and our team helps verify accounts and guide them through placing orders, so kids and caregivers can easily send photos to their family members behind the wall.

Visit Prison Fellowship→

Social Enterprise Alliance

Pelipost is a proud member of the Social Enterprise Alliance, a national network of mission-driven businesses working to create lasting social impact. Being part of SEA reinforces our commitment to doing business in a way that prioritizes people over profit, and puts the families we serve at the center of every decision we make.

Visit Social Enterprise Alliance→

Wire of Hope

Wire of Hope was founded on the same belief that drives Pelipost: that communication with incarcerated loved ones matters, and that letters and photographs have the power to change lives on the inside. We work together to make writing to a pen pal inside easier, because everyone deserves to be remembered.
