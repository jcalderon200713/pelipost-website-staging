Pelipost Products

Everything You Need to Stay Connected

From photos to letters to greeting cards, find the perfect way to reach your incarcerated loved one.

Send

Real mail, printed by us, delivered to your loved one at any correctional facility in the United States.

Photo Prints

From $0.75

Holding a photograph feels different. It pulls a memory close, makes a face familiar again, and reminds someone they're loved across any distance. Upload yours from your phone and we'll take it from there.

Postcards

From $2.49

A quick hello. A thinking of you. A reminder that someone out here is keeping score of the good days. Full-color postcards with your personal message on the back. Simple, affordable, and always a welcome sight in the mail.

Letters

From $2.99

A letter is a piece of home you can hold. Write up to 2,000 characters of updates, memories, and the everyday moments that mean the most, then personalize it with custom fonts, emojis, or a photo.

Greeting Cards

From $3.99

Birthdays, holidays, Tuesdays that need a little extra love. Choose from our collection or customize your own. A card says what everyday mail can't, and gives them something to hold onto long after it arrives.

Activity Letters

From $2.99

Real conversation, delivered. You fill out the first copy with a note, a question, or a story about yourself, and we print and mail it to your loved one. A blank copy is included for them to fill out and send right back to you.
